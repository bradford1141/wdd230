White Space and Clean Design

The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints

churchofjesuschrist.org

The thing I like about the churches webpage is the whitespapce between the photos. It devides the pictures and content nicely. There is also even white space between all the pictures. the white space in the navigation between the search bar, the globe, and the log in are all nicely spaced as well.

PARC: Alignment

Good Books

goodbooks.com

This is harder to see on the phone view, but this website has very nice alignment. All the books are in a line and evenly spaced. In the mobile view, all the books go down the screen in the center and are spaced evenly with a little shadow. On the computer view they are lined up perfectly with nice gaps between them.

PARC: Contrast

Amazon

amazon.com

I like the contrast amazon uses on their website. It ahs a green color at top that dims to a lower color. There is a nice tough of orange to make a pop on the page. The ad's on the front screen are filled with color and contrast to draw attention, this sepecific one is pink. The green gives good contrast to the white page as well.
