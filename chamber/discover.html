This is what the beautiful Coruscant looks like at night. The moon shines bright and you are able to see stars from all across the galaxy. The lights from the tall buildings light up the skyline. In the distance you are able to see the other planets in the galaxy. This is the captial of the galaxy and is where the Jedi Temple resides.

Coruscant during the day is a beautiful place. It is sunny and is fun to look at the passing by ships. The buildings have a unique shape to them that make them stand out. There are lots of big things that go on around Coruscant. This allows for the city to have a night life but also a day life for those who work.

On this planet there is a beautiful sunset. It leaves the sky in a perfect orange and purple color. You are able to see the perfect fly pattern that is formed in the sky. This allows you to see all different types of ships flying over the city. You are able to look at the city from the sky when you take a flight tour of the city.

While going through the city you are able to find that some important people have visited here before. This includes C3PO, R2D2, and BB8 from the republic. You are able to find action figures for all these droids in many of the shops. You can also find a clone, Darth Vader, and Boba Fett.

There are also available pins for those who want them. You can get a pin to show off your favorite person, or collect them all. These pins include a Star Fighter Ship (The Falcon), C3PO, Jango Fett, R2D2, a clone, Death Star, Red light Saber, Green light Saber, Chewy, Princess Leah, and Darth Vader.
